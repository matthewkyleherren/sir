Real Flavor
Starts Small
Made in small batches with fire-roasted tomatoes, warm garam masala, and fresh cream. We don’t rush flavor — we build it, slowly and purposefully, just like it’s done in real family kitchens.
Stirred,
Not Rushed
Our sauce is slow-simmered, stirred with care. This gives spices time to bloom, tomatoes time to deepen, and every bite time to become something more than just a condiment.
