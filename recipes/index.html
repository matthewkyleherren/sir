Easy Chicken Tandoori
Bold, oven-charred chicken in just 20 minutes. Marinated in rich Hungry Tiger flavor.
the breakdown
prep time
10 min
cook time
10 min
serves
2
total
~20 min
ingredients
10 oz Chicken Thigh, cut into 1-inch pieces
1 Lime (zested and halved)
Scallions (thinly sliced)
½ jar Hungry Tiger Tikka Masala Sauce
Fresh Cilantro (for garnish)
Marinate the chicken for extra depth of flavor. Charred edges = oven magic.
instructions
1
Preheat the oven

Preheat your oven to 500°F (260°C).

2
Prepare ingredients

Thinly slice the scallions. Zest the lime and cut it in half.

3
Marinate the chicken

Toss the chicken with Hungry Tiger sauce and marinate for 30 min.

4
Prepare Baking Sheet

Lightly oil a baking sheet.

5
Bake Chicken

Arrange the chicken on the baking sheet. Bake 7–10 minutes, rotating halfway, until cooked and slightly charred.

6
Plate and Garnish

Plate the chicken with lime halves. Garnish with lime zest, scallions, and cilantro.

7
Enjoy!

Squeeze lime over before eating.

Chicken Tikka Masala with Rice
A comforting classic with tender chicken and rice. Simmered in bold, creamy masala sauce.
the breakdown
prep time
10 min
cook time
20 min
serves
2
total
30 min
ingredients
8 oz chopped chicken breast or thigh
1⁄2 cup rice
3⁄4 cup water
1 small onion (use half)
1 green pepper (optional for extra flavor/crunch)
1⁄2 jar Hungry Tiger Tikka Masala Sauce
Fresh cilantro (roughly chopped)
1 tbsp butter
Salt & pepper to taste
Cooking oil
Add green chili for heat or lime for brightness. Great over rice, naan, or even solo.
instructions
1
Prep the Ingredients
  • Wash and dry all produce.
  • Peel and halve the onion; thinly slice one half (use whole onion if doubling recipe).
  • Roughly chop the cilantro.
  • Pat the chicken dry with paper towels. Season with salt and pepper.
2
Cook the Rice

In a small pot, combine:

  • 1⁄2 cup rice
  • 3⁄4 cup water
  • Pinch of salt
  • Bring to a boil.

Once boiling, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15–18 minutes, until rice is tender.

Remove from heat and keep covered until ready to serve.

3
Cook the Chicken, Onion, and Green Pepper
  • Heat a large drizzle of oil in a large pan over medium-high heat.
  • Add sliced onion and a pinch of salt. Cook until onion is slightly softened (about 2–3 minutes).
  • Add chopped green pepper and cook for 1–2 minutes until slightly softened.
  • Add seasoned chicken to the pan.
  • Cook until browned all over and fully cooked (5–8 minutes, until internal temp reaches 165°F/74°C).
4
Add the Sauce
  • Stir in half a jar of Hungry Tiger Tikka Masala Sauce.
  • Cook while stirring for about 2 minutes, until fragrant.
  • Reduce heat to medium-low and let simmer for 1–2 minutes, until sauce thickens and chicken is fully cooked. (If sauce is too thick, add a splash or two of water.)
  • Stir in half of the chopped cilantro.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Turn off heat.
5
Finish the Rice
  • Fluff the cooked rice with a fork.
  • Stir in 1 tbsp butter.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
6
Serve
  • Divide the buttered rice between plates or shallow bowls.
  • Top with chicken tikka masala.
  • Garnish with remaining cilantro.
  • Optionally, sprinkle a pinch of green chili for heat.

Paneer Tikka Masala with Rice
Soft paneer meets fragrant masala and buttered rice. A cozy vegetarian favorite, ready fast.
the breakdown
prep time
10 min
cook time
18-20 min
serves
2
total
~ 30 min
ingredients
8 oz paneer, chopped into bite-sized pieces
1⁄2 cup rice
3⁄4 cup water
1 small onion (use half)
1 green pepper (optional for extra flavor/crunch)
1⁄2 jar Hungry Tiger Tikka Masala Sauce
Fresh cilantro (roughly chopped)
1 tbsp butter
Salt & pepper to taste
Cooking oil
Stir paneer gently to keep it from crumbling. Pairs beautifully with buttered rice.
instructions
1
Prep the Ingredients
  • Wash and dry all produce.
  • Peel and halve the onion; thinly slice one half (use whole onion if doubling recipe).
  • Roughly chop the cilantro.
  • Chop paneer into 1/4 inch cubes.
2
Cook the Rice

In a small pot, combine:

  • 1⁄2 cup rice
  • 3⁄4 cup water
  • Pinch of salt
  • Bring to a boil.

Once boiling, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15–18 minutes, until rice istender.

Remove from heat and keep covered until ready to serve.

3
Cook the Paneer, Onion, and Green Pepper
  • Heat a large drizzle of oil in a large pan over medium-high heat.
  • Add sliced onion and a pinch of salt. Cook until onion is slightly softened (about 2–3 minutes).
  • Add chopped green pepper and cook for 1–2 minutes until slightly softened.
  • Add paneer to the pan.
  • Cook for 1–2 minutes until paneer is lightly golden and heated through, stirring gently to avoid breaking.
4
Add the Sauce
  • Stir in half a jar of Hungry Tiger Tikka Masala Sauce.
  • Cook while stirring for about 2 minutes, until fragrant.
  • Reduce heat to medium-low and let simmer for 1–2 minutes, until sauce thickens and paneer is fully coated. (If sauce is too thick, add a splash or two of water.)
  • Stir in half of the chopped cilantro.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Turn off heat.
5
Finish the Rice
  • Fluff the cooked rice with a fork.
  • Stir in 1 tbsp butter.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
6
Serve
  • Divide the buttered rice between plates or shallow bowls.
  • Top with paneer masala.
  • Garnish with remaining cilantro.
  • Optionally, sprinkle a pinch of green chili for heat.

Chicken Tikka Masala Penne Pasta
Indian spice meets Italian comfort. Pasta coated in creamy tikka richness.
the breakdown
prep time
10 min
cook time
20-25 min
serves
2
total
~ 30-35 min
ingredients
8 oz chopped chicken breast or thigh
4 oz (about 1 cup) penne pasta
1 small onion (use half)
1 green pepper (for flavor and crunch)
1⁄2 jar Hungry Tiger Tikka Masala Sauce
Fresh cilantro (roughly chopped)
1 tbsp butter
Salt & pepper to taste
Cooking oil
Optional: pinch of green chili for garnish
Save pasta water to loosen the sauce. Garnish with cilantro or chili for extra punch.
instructions
1
Prep the Ingredients
  • Wash and dry all produce.
  • Peel and halve the onion; thinly slice one half.
  • Roughly chop the cilantro.
  • Pat the chicken dry with paper towels and season with salt and pepper.
  • Chop the green pepper.
2
Cook the Pasta
  • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  • Add penne pasta and cook according to package instructions (usually 10–12 minutes) until al dente.
  • Drain and set aside 1/4 cup of pasta water.
3
Cook the Chicken, Onion, and Green Pepper
  • Heat a large drizzle of oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
  • Add sliced onion and a pinch of salt; cook 2–3 minutes until softened.
  • Add chopped green pepper; cook 1–2 minutes until softened.
  • Add chicken and cook 5–8 minutes until browned and fully cooked (internal temp 165°F/74°C).
4
Add the Sauce
  • Stir in half the jar of Hungry Tiger Tikka Masala Sauce.
  • Cook, stirring for 2 minutes until fragrant.
  • Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer 1–2 minutes until sauce thickens and chicken is fully coated.
  • If sauce is too thick, add a splash of pasta cooking water to loosen.
  • Stir in half the chopped cilantro.
  • Season with salt and pepper. Turn off heat.
5
Combine Pasta and Sauce
  • Add the cooked penne pasta to the pan with the chicken tikka masala sauce.
  • Toss gently to combine and coat the pasta evenly.
6
Finish and Serve
  • Stir in butter.
  • Taste and adjust salt and pepper if needed.
  • Serve hot, garnished with remaining cilantro and optional pinch of green chili.

Green Pepper & Onion Tikka Masala Pizza
Cheesy, crispy, and full of bold flavor. Topped with veggies and Hungry Tiger sauce.
the breakdown
prep time
10-15 min
cook time
12-15 min
serves
2
total
~ 25-30 min
ingredients
1 small onion, thinly sliced
1 green pepper, chopped
1⁄2 jar Hungry Tiger Tikka Masala Sauce
1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade, enough for 1 medium pizza)
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese (or your favorite melting cheese)
1 tbsp cooking oil
Fresh cilantro, roughly chopped (for garnish)
Optional: Pinch of green chili flakes (for heat)
Salt & pepper to taste
Pre-cook veggies for deeper flavor. Finish with fresh cilantro just before serving.
instructions
1
Prep the Ingredients
  • Wash and dry all produce.
  • Thinly slice the onion and chop the green pepper.
  • Roughly chop cilantro for garnish.
2
Preheat the Oven
  • Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).
  • If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to preheat.
3
Cook the Veggies
  • Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
  • Add sliced onion with a pinch of salt; cook for 2–3 minutes until slightly softened.
  • Add chopped green pepper and cook for 1–2 minutes more.
  • Remove from heat.
4
Prepare the Pizza Base
  • Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
5
Assemble the Pizza
  • Spread 1⁄2 jar of Hungry Tiger Tikka Masala Sauce evenly over the dough.
  • Evenly distribute the cooked onion and green pepper mixture on top.
  • Sprinkle with shredded mozzarella cheese.
6
Bake the Pizza
  • Transfer pizza to the oven on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  • Bake for 12–15 minutes, until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly and melted.
7
Finish and Serve
  • Remove from oven.
  • Garnish with fresh cilantro and optional green chili flakes.
  • Slice and serve hot.
